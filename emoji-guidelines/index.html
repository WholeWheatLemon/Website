pixel emojis are SERIOUS business! it's important you know what you're doing when you make them!


here are my basic principles of emojis:


1. emojis show emotion!

this is a simple one but make sure you remember it! no matter if the emotion is more clear like a grin , or more abstract like a skull , emojis exist to show emotion! make sure you have a clear idea of what emotion you're trying to portray.



2. stay consistent with your style (sometimes!)

you should have a consistent style with most of your emojis.
